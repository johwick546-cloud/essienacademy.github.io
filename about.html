Our Mission & Values

Essien's Excellence Academy was founded on the principle that every child deserves a Christ-centered education that nurtures both academic excellence and moral character. Our mission is to develop the next generation of leaders through Christlike behavior, academic rigor, and a commitment to service.

"Rest not; aspire to a greater height. These words guide everything we do at Essien's Excellence Academy. We believe that with faith, perseverance, and the right educational foundation, every student can achieve their God-given potential."

- Founder's Vision

We integrate Christian values throughout our curriculum and school culture, creating an environment where students can grow spiritually while excelling academically. Our approach to education focuses on developing the whole child - mind, body, and spirit.

Our History

2005

Essien's Excellence Academy was founded with just 35 students and 3 teachers, operating from a small building in the community.

2010

We moved to our current campus, with facilities for both elementary and junior high students, and enrollment grew to 200 students.

2015

Celebrated our 10th anniversary and introduced the STEM program, focusing on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

2020

Despite global challenges, we successfully transitioned to hybrid learning and expanded our digital infrastructure.

2025

Currently serving over 500 students with a faculty of 35 dedicated teachers and staff members.

Admissions Process

Begin your journey to excellence at Essien's Excellence Academy

Our Admissions Process

Inquiry

Submit an online inquiry form or visit our campus to learn more about our programs and community.

Application

Complete our online application form and submit all required documentation.

Assessment

Students will be invited for an assessment to determine appropriate grade placement.

Interview

Families will meet with our admissions team for an interview and campus tour.

Acceptance

Receive your admission decision and welcome package within 2 weeks of assessment.

Enrollment

Complete enrollment paperwork and submit tuition payment to secure your spot.

Admissions Requirements

Elementary School (P1-P3)

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Previous school records (if applicable)

Upper Elementary (P4-P6)

  • All elementary requirements
  • Academic transcripts from previous school
  • Standardized test results (if available)
  • Teacher recommendation form
  • Student writing sample

Junior High (JHS 1-3)

  • All upper elementary requirements
  • Complete academic records
  • Mathematics and English placement tests
  • Two teacher recommendations
  • Personal statement from student

Admissions Process

Begin your journey to excellence at Essien's Excellence Academy

Our Admissions Process

Inquiry

Submit an online inquiry form or visit our campus to learn more about our programs and community.

Application

Complete our online application form and submit all required documentation.

Assessment

Students will be invited for an assessment to determine appropriate grade placement.

Interview

Families will meet with our admissions team for an interview and campus tour.

Acceptance

Receive your admission decision and welcome package within 2 weeks of assessment.

Enrollment

Complete enrollment paperwork and submit tuition payment to secure your spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline? +
We have rolling admissions, but priority is given to applications received before March 31st for the following academic year.
Is financial aid available? +
Yes, we offer limited financial aid based on need and academic merit. Please contact our admissions office for more information.
What are the school hours? +
School runs from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM Monday through Friday. After-school programs are available until 5:30 PM.
Do you offer transportation services? +
Yes, we have bus services available for students living within a 15km radius of the school.
What is the student-teacher ratio? +
We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention for each student.

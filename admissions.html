Admissions

Thank you for your interest in Essien's Excellence Academy. We are excited to welcome new students and families into our Christ-centered learning community.

Application Process

  1. Complete the application form (available at the school office or online).
  2. Submit the required documents, including a copy of the student’s birth certificate and previous school records.
  3. Schedule an entrance assessment and interview.
  4. Receive an admission decision from the school.

Requirements

Applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Most recent report card or transcript

Important Dates

Admissions for the 2025–2026 academic year are now open. Spaces are limited, so early applications are encouraged.
